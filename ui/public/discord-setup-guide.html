Discord Setup Guide

How to create a Discord bot and connect it to Adjutant so you can @mention it in any server channel.

🎮 Discord

Step 1 — Create a Discord Application

  1. 1

    Go to discord.com/developers/applications and sign in.

  2. 2

    Click New Application, name it (e.g. Adjutant), and click Create.

Step 2 — Configure the Bot

  1. 1

    In the left sidebar, click Bot.

  2. 2

    Under Privileged Gateway Intents, enable Message Content Intent — this is required for the bot to read message text.

  3. 3

    Click Save Changes.

  4. 4

    Click Reset Token, confirm, and copy your bot token.

Step 3 — Generate an Invite URL

  1. 1

    In the left sidebar, expand OAuth2 and click URL Generator.

  2. 2

    Under Scopes, check bot.

  3. 3

    Under Bot Permissions, check:

    • View Channels
    • Send Messages
    • Read Message History
    • Use Slash Commands

  4. 4

    Copy the generated URL and open it in your browser to invite the bot to your server.

Step 4 — Create a Notification Channel

  1. 1

    In your Discord server, create a text channel (e.g. #adjutant-notifications).

  2. 2

    Make sure the bot has access: right-click the channel → Edit Channel → Permissions → add your bot with Send Messages permission.

Step 5 — Connect in Adjutant

Bot Token→paste into the Bot Token field
Then: click Connect Discord, and once connected select your Notification Channel from the dropdown. @mention your bot in any channel to start sending directives.
